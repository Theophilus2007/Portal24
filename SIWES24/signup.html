Applying to Federal College of Animal Health and Production Technology

Application Requirement

- Select program (ND or HND), program type (Full-time or Part-time) and course
- Sign up by filling the sign-up form and click on submit
Please note:
ensure you use functioning phone number and email address that is valid, accessible and functioning.

- After submission a verification mail will be sent to your mail
- Click the link in the verification mail. You will be redirected to the login page, enter your email/jamb registration number and password.
- Pay the required Post-UTME fee of 2,000naira (for National diploma candidates) and 10,000naira (for Higher national diploma candidates)
- Complete the biodata form and upload all necessary documents
- Print out the photo card and bring it along on the post UTME/screening day. This is what will be used to admit candidate into the examination.
- Continue to check your email for invitation for post-UTME exam/screening.
